Shares structure

All companies must have at least 1 share. Apart from that, you can structure things in whatever way you like. Issued shares are shares that will be distributed from day 1. Unissued shares are shares that you can distribute later on, i.e. to future team members or investors. The value per share represents your personal liability, so, if you wish to reduce risks, just pick the smallest number.
